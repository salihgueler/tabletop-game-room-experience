# Product

Tabletop Game Room Experience is a 16-bit pixel-art tabletop RPG room. Players sign in, create a hero from fantasy character sprites, browse or create campaigns in the Guild Hall, then play turn-based sessions with a 4-seat party.

Core experience:

- Authenticated users create persistent characters across Paladin, Sorcerer, Rogue, and Ranger classes.
- The Guild Hall supports public games, private games by access code, AI-filled parties, and waiting for human players.
- The Game Room shows turn order, the dungeon board, action choices, dice results, inventory, chat, and streamed AI reasoning.
- Sessions are server-authoritative and time-limited; the client displays state but does not invent game outcomes.

AI is central to the product: an AI Dungeon Master narrates the scene and action outcomes, while AI companions can fill party seats and take turns in character. Realtime updates keep state, chat, and AI thinking synchronized across connected players.
